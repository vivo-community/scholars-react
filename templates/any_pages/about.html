About VIVO Scholar

Developed by the VIVO Scholar Task Force, VIVO Scholar is an open-source tool that showcases the scholarly contributions and activities of the VIVO community. It includes up-to-date information on scholars' publications, grants, and contact information. VIVO Scholar serves as a read-only user interface for browsing data compiled in VIVO, providing a developer-friendly means of customization and implementation for institutions. Want to learn more about VIVO Scholar and how you can adopt it at your in- stituation? See our FAQ or check out the VIVO Scholar Task Force Wiki

New Features

  • Intuitive, responsive, customizable, and user-friendly interface
  • Enhanced search functions to narrow results with multi-level faceting
  • GraphQL endpoint that enables feeding data to other websites

Search Tips

  • Keep your search simple. Use short, single terms unless your searches are returning too many results. Use the filters on the results page to further narrow your search.
  • Searches are not case-sensitive
  • Use the wildcard * character to match an even wider variation; e.g. nano* will match both nanotechnology and nanofabrication
